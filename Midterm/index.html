Welcome to East High!

Who We Are

East high is an elite private high school in beautiful Salt Lake City. East high has a little something for everyone. We have classes on everything from european history to pre-calculus. Looking for extra curricular activities? East high has a nationally acclaimed musical theatre club, as well as a competitive basketball team. Go Wildcats! For more information you can visit About Us.

Our Community

We at East High work hard to foster our community. Our principal, guidance counselors, heck, even our janitors join in for our famous school flash mob dance numbers. Whoever you are, you'll find a place at our school. We're sure of it.
