Classes

Arts
  • Fine Art
  • Photography
  • Theatre
  • Choir
  • Ceramics
  • Band
East High prides itself in having one of the top art programs in the country. We highly value the arts, and encourage students to express themselves artistically - even if that means breaking out into song in the middle of an exam! In fact, each student gets a full time choreographer, vocal coach, and a band that follows them anywhere they go just in case they ever feel in the mood for music! Our school has two annual musicals, and they're some of the biggest events of the year - everyone in the school community comes to watch!
While the performing arts is an integral aspect of everyday life at East High, we also have great course offerings for those interested in fine arts. You'd get the very special job of painting the sets for our biannual musicals! However we'd really prefer if you take a class that would be of more use to us like dancing - we really could use more backup dancers to support the main characters' musical numbers.
Humanities
  • Literature
  • Language and Literature
  • Media Studies
  • Creative Writing
  • Poetry and Lyricism
  • Theatrical Literature
The humanities are valued highly here at East High. Of course, not as highly as the performing arts, but we do take certain measures to ensure we're applying actually important concepts into our humanities courses as well. For example, in our Theatrical Literature course, the students go onstage to act out whichever playtext they're studying. In Media Studies, you get to spread the word about our biannual musicals - and you might even get to interview one of the main characters!
It was Shakespeare who once said "To be, or not to be, that is the question: Whether 'tis nobler in the mind to suffer the slings and arrows of outrageous fortune, or to take arms against a sea of troubles and by opposing end them." Do you know what that means? Do you even want to know? Me neither! That's why we offer actually interesting topics in our classes, like how to write original songs when you're dealing with high school heartbreak.
Natural Sciences
  • Chemistry
  • Biology
  • Physics
  • Design Technology
  • Ecology
  • Health Science
The natural sciences are a greatly appreciated field here at East High. If it weren't for the scientific innovation of the past few centuries, we wouldn't have modern theatre technology! How would we set the stage alight with an explosion of multi-colored lasers for the dance sequence in the second act of our 2005 fall musical: Disco Dan and his Crazy Crew? How would we project live footage of a bustling city in the spring sequel, Disco Dan: Lost in New York?
We encourage our students to take our offered natural science courses to discover new ways of innovating the theatrical experience. As Einstein once said "E = mc2." I'm not sure what he originally meant by that, but at East High we say it means Energizing theatre = musicality * character development2 (the squared means we need at least two character arcs - just one is lazy scriptwriting!).
Mathematics
  • Statistics
  • Calculus
  • Linear Algebra
  • Trigonometry
  • Discrete Mathematics
  • Probability Theory
At East High, we have an amazing math department. We offer a wide range of math courses, from Statistics where you process and analyze data from our biannual musical ticket sales to determine which factors increase revenue, to Trigonometry where you can learn how to draw a triangle! This was a very useful skill for our set and costume designers in our 2004 hit show, Trent the Triangle and his Geometric Gang!
We also offer Probability Theory, and if you take it you'll discover that there's a 110% chance you'll love it here at East High! Even if math isn't your strong suit, we have tons of tutors that will teach you how to sing - that way, you can pursue a passion in music and you won't need silly things like Calculus!
Social Sciences
  • Psychology
  • Economics
  • Geography
  • Global Politics
  • World History
  • US History
The Social Sciences are incredibly important at East High. We offer a wide range of classes, from World History where you can learn about musical theatre across the globe, or US history if you're just into Broadway.
No matter what you're interested in, you're sure to find a social science class that you'll enjoy. The classes are very collaborative, and involve lectures, discussions, and student presentations where you can get hands-on expereince with public speaking.
Language Acquisition
  • Spanish
  • French
  • Portugese
  • Mandarin
  • Latin
  • Japanese
East High prides itself in its international community, and it allows us to offer a wide range of language classes, from Spanish to Latin to Mandarin.
We also offer field trips to various countries so that our students can engage with native speakers. It's one of the highlights of the school year, aside from our biannual school musicals!
