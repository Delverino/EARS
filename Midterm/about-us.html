About Us

Story of EastHigh

Founded by Mr. Mystery in 1984, EastHigh is one of the coed instituions that offers elite education for high school students. Mr. Mystery loves wildcats, that's why our school mascot is wildcat. Now EastHigh is home for more than 8000 students from 30 different countries. With a student-faculty ratio of 7:1, we provide students with opportunities to explore their area of interests.

Our Mission

Fostering a community of creative thinkers, caring for our communities and the world.
