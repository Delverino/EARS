Admissions FAQs

When will I be notified of the admissions decision??
We will release our decision after January 31th on a rolling basis.

Is it important to visit EastHigh during the admission process?
Yes, it's important for us to interview applicants and for students to explore our campus.

Where do the teachers come from?
The majority of our teachers are from North America, and we also have teachers from the UK, France, Japan, Australia and so on.

What if I missed the application deadline?
Unfortunately, late applications won't be accepeted, we are sorry about that.

Are sports, arts, and music matter?
Absolutely! Students are encouraged to elaborate on their experience in these areas in the application process.

What kinds of decisions will I possibly get?
Acceptance, Refusal, and Waiting List.

How should I interpret being offered on waitlist?
Being on the waitlist means that the candidates have the qualities to be admitted but we are currently unable to offer places for various reason, such as the class is full .

Do interviews matter?
Yes, candidates are encouraged to schedule an interview during their campus visit, either with our admission member or an alumni.
