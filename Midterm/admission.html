Admission

Become an EastHigh student

Apply Online: We are using a rolling admission system. Our application for 2022-2023 is now available on the portal!
Application materials: All application materials must be submitted via the portal.
Apply for financial aid: We offer $10 million annually in need-based financial aid.

We do not discriminate on the basis of race, color, religion, gender, sexual orientation, national origin, or any disabilities in the admission process.

What is the profile of a future EastHigh student?

Caring, Creative and Collaborative:
While Tufts students are as multidimensional as the Hill they call home, these descriptors tend to define our community. Located just five miles outside of Boston, Tufts values close student-faculty collaboration, unique interdisciplinary research, and diversities of all kinds.

Admissions FAQs

How long does it take to get a decision:
We will release our decision after 31 January on a rolling basis.

Is it important to visit EastHigh during the admission process:
Yes, it's important for us to interview applicants and for students to explore our campus.
