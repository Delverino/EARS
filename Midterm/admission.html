Admission

Become one of the Wildcats!

Apply Online: We are using a rolling admission system. Our application for 2022-2023 is now available on the portal!
Application Deadline: 01/15
Application materials: Standardized Testings, Essays, Recommendations. All application materials must be submitted via the portal.
Tuition and fee:

Cost
Tuition $53,350
Books and supplies (estimated) $525
Total $53875

Apply for financial aid: We offer $10 million annually in need-based financial aid.

We do not discriminate on the basis of race, color, religion, gender, sexual orientation, nationality, or any disabilities in the admission process.


What is the profile of a future East High student?

Caring, Creative and Collaborative:
We expect our students not only to perform well in academics, but also be caring about others. In addition, to be creative in different areas, such as music, art and sports. Most importantly, we value collaboration over competition.

Admissions FAQs

When will I be notified of the admissions decision?
We will release our decision after January 31th on a rolling basis.

Is it important to visit East High during the admission process?
Yes, it's important for us to interview applicants and for students to explore our campus.

Where do the teachers come from?
The majority of our teachers are from North America, and we also have teachers from the UK, France, Japan, Australia and so on.

What if I missed the application deadline?
Unfortunately, late applications won't be accepeted, we are sorry about that.

Do sports, arts, and music matter?
Absolutely! Students are encouraged to elaborate on their experience in these areas in the application process.

What kinds of decisions will I possibly get?
Acceptance, Refusal, and Waiting List.

How should I interpret being put on the waitlist?
Being on the waitlist means that the candidates have the qualities to be admitted but we are currently unable to offer places for various reasons, like if the class is full .

Do interviews matter?
Yes, candidates are encouraged to schedule an interview during their campus visit, either with our admission member or an alumni.
